Brief Introduction of Prof.Ning Li

Ning Li is currently a Professor with the Department of Automation, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She received the Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2002. From 2002 to 2005, she was an Associate Professor with the Department of Automation, East China University of Science and Technology. Then she joined Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She worked in Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) during February to May in 2009 and National University of Singapore (NUS) during July to November in 2011 as a visiting scholar. Her research focuses on Complex System Modeling and Control, Artificial Intelligence and Big Data Analysis. She is the second author of the book Fuzzy Predictive Control and Applications of Complex Systems. She has published over 100 papers and 7 patents and Software Copyright Registration. She won one First Prize of Shanghai Science and Technology Progress Award and two First Prizes of Shanghai Teaching Award. She is a committee member of Shanghai Automation Society.
